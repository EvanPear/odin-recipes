Spicy Thai Basil Chicken (Pad Krapow Gai)

What Is Thai Basil Chicken

Thai Basil Chicken is a stir-fry made with finely chopped or ground chicken, cooked with chili peppers, garlic, and shallots. It's flavored with a sweet and salty sauce and finished with fresh basil, which adds a nice, fragrant touch. This dish is a staple in Thai cuisine, often found in restaurants and as a popular street food, typically served over steamed rice.

Ingredients for Thai Basil Chicken

  • 2/3 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce, or as needed
  • 4 teaspoons fish sauce
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons brown sugar
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 pound skinless, boneless chicken thighs, coarsely chopped
  • 1/2 cup sliced shallots
  • 8 gloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons minced Thai chilies, serrano, or other hot pepper
  • 2 cups very thinly sliced fresh basil leaves
  • 1 quart hot cooked rice
